    • 1. Tigers have been around for quite a while, being around about 2 million years ago.


    2.) Over the last 150 years, the tigers' range has shrunk by nearly 95%. There are estimated to be around 3,900 tigers left in the wild.


    3.) There are more tigers in captivity in the US than are left in the wild.


    4.) India is the country with the largest number of Wild Tigers, with around 3,000.


    5.) Tigers don't usually eat their prey at the kill site - but instead drag their prey into cover to feed. If a tiger leaves - say to get a drink - it will cover it's kill by raking leaves, dirt, grass and even rocks over the carcass.
